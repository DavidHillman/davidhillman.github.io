I came, was given a name, and told to go forth and conquer. After many attempts at conquering the world, I have now settled for conquering myself and my bad habits. While I can get excited and talk a lot, I do listen if you ask me nicely. I enjoy being around people who are outgoing, non-judgemental, and just great traveling companions. I don't like squares because they are pointy. Minimalism is my motto. Bicycles are how I get around. Let's keep things simple, stupid and organic shall we?
